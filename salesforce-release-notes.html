SKQx Beta Test

  • This feature is an add on to the Sonic Disaster Relief grant type to accommodate the necessary fields and questions to run a beta test with SKQx. Our Sonic employees who are affect by a disaster will benefit from having this feature because they will be able to receive their funds faster. Also, the accounting team will benefit from this because there will be less, they would need to process. This addition solves the problem of mailing individual checks to our team members.
  • What you will see be different
    • You will see the addition of a question on the application about the team member having a smart phone and if they would like to participate in the test. You will also see eight new fields on the funding request and six new fields on the disbursement.
